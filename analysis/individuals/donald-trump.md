# Donald Trump - The Attractor

## Role in Tech Realignment
Not a tech figure himself, but the gravitational center of the political realignment. Understanding his appeal to tech leaders is essential context.

## Background
- Born: 1946, New York
- Career: Real estate, media, politics
- President: 2017-2021, 2025-present

## Historical Tech Relationship

### Pre-2015: Casual Connections
- Celebrity figure known to tech elites
- Appeared at tech events occasionally
- No significant political tech relationship

### 2015-2016: Campaign & Opposition
- Announced candidacy - tech industry largely dismissive
- Peter Thiel only major tech supporter
- Silicon Valley overwhelmingly backed Clinton
- Tech workers donated 60:1 to Clinton vs Trump

### 2017-2021: First Term - Adversarial
- Tech leaders met at Trump Tower (reluctantly)
- Travel ban sparked tech opposition
- Ongoing conflicts over content moderation
- Trump banned from Twitter, Facebook after Jan 6

### 2021-2024: Out of Office - Courtship
- Created Truth Social (competitor to Twitter)
- Began criticizing "Big Tech censorship" less as Musk shifted Twitter
- Cultivated relationships with crypto industry
- Softened rhetoric as tech leaders approached

### 2025: Second Term - Alliance
- Tech CEOs at inauguration in prominent positions
- Musk given government role (DOGE)
- Immediate executive orders favorable to tech
- Antitrust pressure appears to ease

## What Trump Offers Tech

### Regulatory Relief
- Reduced antitrust enforcement
- Lighter touch on AI regulation
- Crypto-friendly SEC appointments
- Rolling back Biden-era tech scrutiny

### Tax Policy
- Corporate tax cuts
- Capital gains treatment
- Carried interest preservation (for VCs)
- State tax arbitrage (no pressure on TX, FL)

### Immigration (Complex)
- H-1B visa support (shifted position)
- Tech worker pathway promises
- Anti-illegal immigration (labor cost implications)

### Culture War Alignment
- Anti-"woke" positioning
- DEI rollback support
- "Free speech" framing on content moderation
- Enemy of tech's internal progressive employees

### Access & Status
- Mar-a-Lago dinners
- Inauguration seating
- Direct phone line to power
- Flattery and recognition

## Key Quotes on Tech

> "We're going to bring the tech companies back" - 2024

> "Elon is a great guy... he's doing an incredible job" - 2024

> "Zuckerberg called me, he said 'I'd like to congratulate you'" - 2024

## The Bargain

Tech leaders appear to have calculated:
1. **Regulatory capture** is worth political realignment
2. **Personal relationships** with Trump provide protection
3. **Cultural war** against employees/left is acceptable cost
4. **Democratic threats** (antitrust, taxes) were greater risk
