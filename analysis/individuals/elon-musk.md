# Elon Musk - Political Evolution

## Background
- Born: 1971, South Africa
- Companies: Tesla, SpaceX, X (Twitter), Neuralink, xAI, The Boring Company
- Net Worth: ~$300B+ (fluctuates, often world's richest)

## Political Timeline

### Pre-2020: Moderate/Libertarian
- Donated to both parties historically
- Supported Obama-era clean energy initiatives
- Generally avoided overt political stances
- Tesla benefited from Democratic clean energy policies

### 2020-2022: Transition Period
- COVID lockdown criticism began shift
- Criticized California regulations
- Moved Tesla HQ from California to Texas
- Growing criticism of "woke" culture

### 2022: Twitter Acquisition
- April 2022: Announced Twitter acquisition
- October 2022: Deal closed
- Immediately began policy changes
- Reinstated banned accounts including Trump
- "Free speech absolutist" framing

### 2023-2024: Full Political Shift
- June 2023: Initially backed DeSantis for president
- After DeSantis dropped out, endorsed Trump
- Became Trump's most prominent tech supporter
- Created America PAC - spent $100M+ on Trump campaign
- Daily presence on X promoting Trump/MAGA content
- Spread election misinformation

### 2025: Government Role
- Named co-lead of "Department of Government Efficiency" (DOGE)
- Present at Trump inauguration in prominent position
- Active role in administration policy
