# Google/Alphabet - The Quiet Retreat

## Company Overview
- Founded: 1998
- Parent Company: Alphabet (2015)
- CEO: Sundar Pichai
- Employees: ~180,000
- Market Cap: ~$2T
- Products: Search, Android, YouTube, Cloud, Gemini AI, Waymo

## The DEI Arc

### 2014-2019: DEI Build-Up

#### Public Commitments
- First diversity report (2014)
- Annual diversity data publication
- $150M diversity initiative (2015)
- Code Next, CS First programs
- Howard West (HBCU partnership at Google campus)
- Hiring goals and targets

#### Internal Culture
- TGIF all-hands with open political discussion
- Employee activism encouraged
- "Don't be evil" ethos
- Strong ERG (Employee Resource Group) culture
- Internal petitions common and respected

#### Major Activist Moments
- **2017**: James Damore memo and firing
  - Engineer wrote anti-diversity memo
  - Fired for "perpetuating harmful stereotypes"
  - Became right-wing cause célèbre

- **2018**: Project Maven walkout
  - Military AI contract cancelled after 4,000+ employee protest
  - Employees successfully killed business deal
  - Peak of internal activist power

- **2018**: Dragonfly protest
  - Censored China search engine cancelled
  - Employee pressure again successful

- **2018**: Walkout over sexual harassment
  - 20,000 employees walked out globally
  - Protesting handling of Andy Rubin payout
  - Leadership pledged changes

### 2020: Peak Moment

#### George Floyd Response
- Sundar email to all employees
- $12M to racial justice organizations
- Internal town halls on racism
- Black@ ERG visibility increased
- Commitment to increase Black leadership

#### COVID Response
- YouTube aggressive on "medical misinformation"
- Demonetization of COVID skeptic content
- Partnership with WHO
- Content moderation expansion

### 2021-2023: The Retreat Begins

#### AI Ethics Team Gutting
- **December 2020**: Timnit Gebru fired
  - Co-lead of AI ethics team
  - Disputed paper on AI bias
  - Massive controversy

- **February 2021**: Margaret Mitchell fired
  - Other co-lead of AI ethics
  - Investigated Gebru firing
  - AI ethics team decimated

#### Layoffs Hit Diversity
- **January 2023**: 12,000 layoffs announced
- DEI team disproportionately cut
- Recruiting diversity team eliminated
- HBCU partnership scaled back

#### Internal Culture Shift
- TGIF meetings reduced in frequency
- Less open political discussion
- Internal activism discouraged
- "Focused" culture messaging
- Memos warning against politics at work

#### YouTube Policy Shifts
- Less aggressive demonetization
- Creator complaints addressed
- Right-wing content less restricted
- "Balance" framing

### 2024-2025: Alignment

#### Political Repositioning
- Pichai Mar-a-Lago meeting with Trump
- Muted election commentary
- AI focus over social issues
- Inauguration attendance

#### DEI Further Cuts
- Remaining diversity programs scaled
- Goals quietly dropped
- Less public reporting
- "Merit" language return

#### Antitrust Considerations
- Lost DOJ monopoly case (2024)
- Remedies pending
- Trump DOJ approach uncertain
- Strong incentive to cultivate relationship

## Activist Power: Rise and Fall

### 2018: Peak Power
| Event | Employee Action | Result |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Project Maven | 4,000+ petition, resignations | Contract cancelled |
| Dragonfly | Internal pressure | Project cancelled |
| Harassment | 20,000 walkout | Policy changes |

### 2023: Power Eliminated
| Event | Employee Action | Result |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Layoff protests | Some internal dissent | Layoffs continued |
| Palestine protests | 50 arrested | Employees fired |
| DEI cuts | Minimal organized response | Cuts continued |

### What Changed
1. **Economic leverage**: Layoffs showed employees replaceable
2. **Leadership shift**: Less tolerant of activism
3. **Activist attrition**: Most vocal left or were fired
4. **Cultural shift**: New hires less activist-oriented
5. **Fear**: Post-layoff, remaining employees cautious

## Project Maven to Defense Expansion

### 2018: Maven Cancelled
- AI for military drone footage analysis
- Employee petition cited ethical concerns
- Google withdrew from contract
- Seen as employee victory

### 2024: Defense Work Expanded
- Cloud contracts with DoD
- Intelligence community partnerships
- Anduril-style defense tech engagement
- Maven protesters largely gone
- No significant internal opposition

## AI Ethics: Before and After

### Ethical AI Team (2018-2020)
- Gebru and Mitchell leading
- Published research on AI bias
- Raised concerns about large language models
- Internal voice for caution

### Post-Purge (2021-present)
- Team gutted
- Remaining members silenced
- Gemini launched despite bias issues
- "Ship fast" mentality
- Ethics as afterthought

## YouTube Specific

### Content Moderation Evolution

#### 2019-2020: Aggressive
- Demonetization waves
- "Adpocalypse" - advertiser pressure
- Right-wing creator complaints
- Steven Crowder controversy
- COVID "misinformation" removal

#### 2024-2025: Loosened
- Less aggressive enforcement
- Creator-friendly policy changes
- Right-wing content thriving
- "Neutrality" framing
- Reduced editorializing

### Political Content Shift
- Right-wing creators report less suppression
- Left-wing creators report algorithm changes
- Joe Rogan, etc. on platform without issues
- "Balance" as cover for rightward shift

## Antitrust Pressure

### DOJ Case (Search)
- Filed: 2020
- Verdict: 2024 - Google found to have illegal monopoly
- Remedies: TBD
  - Could require Chrome/Android divestiture
  - Default search deal changes
  - Structural remedies possible

### Implications
- Trump DOJ approach uncertain
- Could settle favorably
- Strong incentive to align with administration
- Policy engagement priority

## Don't Be Evil → ...

### The Slogan Evolution
- 2000s: "Don't be evil" - corporate motto
- 2015: Restructuring - Alphabet's code of conduct changes
- 2018: Removed from preface of code of conduct
- Now: Largely memory

### What It Meant
- Employee belief in ethical tech
- Willingness to sacrifice profit for principle
- Maven, Dragonfly cancellations
- Now: Historical artifact
